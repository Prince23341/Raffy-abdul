Available Strands

Explore the academic strands we offer to help you achieve your future goals.

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Ideal for students interested in pursuing careers in engineering, medicine, and other technical fields. The STEM strand prepares you with strong foundations in science and mathematics.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

For future business leaders and entrepreneurs, the ABM strand provides knowledge in management, finance, and entrepreneurship to prepare you for the corporate world.

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

Focused on students interested in the arts, literature, and social sciences. The HUMSS strand nurtures critical thinking and communication skills for future careers in education, journalism, and more.

GAS (General Academic Strand)

The GAS strand is for students who want a balanced academic experience. It offers a mix of subjects from other strands, making it ideal for those still exploring their career options.

TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood)

The TVL strand offers practical skills in areas like ICT, Home Economics, and Industrial Arts. It’s perfect for students who want hands-on learning and a fast track to employment.

ICT (Information and Communications Technology)

The ICT strand equips students with skills in software, web development, and digital communication. It’s an ideal choice for those interested in technology-driven careers in IT, programming, and multimedia.

EIM (Electrical Installation and Maintenance)

The EIM strand provides knowledge and hands-on skills in electrical installation and maintenance. It prepares students for careers in the electrical industry with strong technical competencies.
